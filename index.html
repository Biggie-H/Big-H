About Big H

Born and brewed in South Africa, Big H is that rapper who can switch from soulful bars to club heat faster than you can say “Aweh!”. His sound is a mix of grit, groove and introspection — versatility in its final form. He’s got jokes, depth, and a catalogue full of emotions with a side of 808s. If hip-hop had a buffet, Big H’s plate would be overflowing.
